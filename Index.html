The Label

Visisongone Records is an independent editing label launched from somewhere the radar can't reach — no major backing, no corporate orbit, no gravity to hold it down.

Founded and led by Harley, the label exists to push creative work beyond every boundary the mainstream dares to draw. We operate at the intersection of visual culture, sound, and deep space attitude.

Every project that comes out of Visisongone carries the same signal: uncompromising vision, precision execution, and zero tolerance for the ordinary.

We don't follow stars. We become them.
